Ingredients Steps

2 cups all purpose flour, sifted

1 cup Sugar

1 tablespoon Baking Powder

1/4 cup Butter, melted

1 piece Egg

1 packet NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream

1 cup Water

1/2 bar cheese, sliced

1/4 cup Butter, melted
Step 1 Sift flour, sugar, and baking powder. Combine melted butter, egg, NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream, and water. Mix in wet ingredients to dry ingredients until smooth.

Step 2 Brush muffin pan with butter. Pour Puto mixture ¾ of the way of the muffin pan. Top with cheese slices. Steam over high heat for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted in the Puto. Set aside to cool completely.

Step 3 Remove from the muffin and brush the pot with melted butter. Serve.
