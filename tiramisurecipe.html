Ingredients Steps

4 oz cream cheese, softened

1/2 cup heavy cream

1/4 cup confectioner's sugar

1 Tbsp bourbon or dark rum

6 ladyfingers (from a 7-oz. pkg., such as Savoiardi), halved crosswise

1/2 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled

1 cup fresh raspberries, divided, plus more for topping

1 oz bittersweet chocolate (from a 3-oz. bar)
Step 1 Beat cream cheese in a medium bowl with an electric mixer on high speed until creamy, about 1 minute. Add heavy cream, sugar, and bourbon; beat on high speed until smooth and thickened, about 2 minutes.

Step 2 Dip 6 ladyfinger halves in espresso for 5 seconds, turning to coat. In each of 2 (12-ounce) straight-sided glasses, place 3 pieces in an even layer. Top with 1/4 cup raspberries. Spoon about 1/3 cup cream cheese mixture over raspberries. Repeat layers, ending with cream cheese mixture. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours and up to 2 days.

Step 3 Grate desired amount of chocolate over top of each tiramisu using a Microplane. Top with raspberries.
